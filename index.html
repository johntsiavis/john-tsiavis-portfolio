About

John Tsiavis is a multifaceted artist whose talents extend across directing brand films, documentaries, TVCs, as well as advertising, entertainment, and fashion photography.

Tsiavis was shortlisted as an Emerging Director at the AWARD Awards 2023 and secured wins for Best Short Director and Best Australian Documentary at the Melbourne Documentary Film Festival in 2022.

His clients include Nintendo, Toyota, Disney, HBO, NBC, SONY, NICKELODEON, FOXTEL/Binge & Paramount+. His work has been published in titles as diverse as Esquire, Rolling Stone, Harper's Bazaar, GQ & Flaunt magazine.

"The portrait photography of John Tsiavis is characterized by a tough clarity of intent and subtle sophistication of expression. His portraits in the collection are compelling precisely for the complexity of human identity they convey."

– Dr Christopher Chapman, Curator, National Portrait Gallery, Australia
